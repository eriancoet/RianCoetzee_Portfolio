Education

High School & DTP Graphic Design 1998 -2000

I finished my National Certificate in Richards Bay, KZN. After finishing my NQF4 I studied DTP and Graphic Design at a Company called Hirt & Carter in Durban. The course was for one year, we covered Adobe Suite. Photoshop, Quark Express, Illustrator and some theory of typography and colours.  1998-2000.  

Multi Media in Durban 2000-2001

When I finished my course I continued studying at Durban Multi-Media Tech where I learned how to use Adobe Premier, Maya 3d Studio, Adobe Director, Macromedia Wavelab for audio and the techniques used for Multi-Media design.  When I finished the course I traveled to Israel. I love travelling and experiencing different cultures. In Israel I worked in a Kibbutz. The Kibbutz made soap, I travelled and lived in Israel for one year.

Sound Engineering Cape Town 2001-2002

 When I came back to South Africa I continued studying in Cape Town while working at a Restaurant as waiter. I studied Sound Engineering, I’m a musician and studied music also. I play the bass guitar and also classical guitar and electric guitar. I'm learning to play the Cello and Piano at the moment. In the Studio we learned to record bands. How a patch-bay works, wavelab for audio editing, the Sound Desk and all its features, and equipment and their uses.

Work Experience

Video Editing/Graphic Design 2002-2003

After I finished the Sound Engineering Course I got my first Job as a Video Editor in a photo Studio in Middelburg. We worked on Safety Material for the Mining Industry. I also edited photos and made some printing material. The videos was induction Videos and safety videos for BHP Billiton. I enjoyed the job as video editor, but I wanted to see more of the world.

I worked in video editing and graphic design as such for 1 years and 4 months at Foto First in Middelburg, but I decided to go to England for 2 years on a Working Holiday.

Sound Room, Legal TV - Birmingham 2003-2005

In the UK I did many odd jobs like gardening, kitchen work and Barman/Waiter positions. I then finally got a job at a TV studio, the TV studio’s name was Legal TV. I worked there as a Camera Operator and also in the Sound Room as Sound Engineer. The studio broadcasted on SKY TV and my job was to mic up the presenters for live talk Shows and also to check their Sound if their EQ is correct and volume. I also had to make recordings on DVD of the Audio and Video tracks which is then kept by the studio and the presenters for their portfolio and so on. I also started doing Web Design in an online Course from a London Company called Redwood College

Freelancing Wedding Videos and 2005-ongoing

I came back from England in 2005 and then I struggled to get a job in the field of my studies. I came to Sabie, Mpumalanga, South Africa and worked with my family in the Spar Supermarket that they owned. I didn’t do anything interesting, mainly paperwork and Goods Received Voucher processing. I then started Freelancing in Video Editing and Videography. 

I did some Wedding Videos and here and there I helped some friends with a Joomla website. I still played music and joined a band. We did some gigs locally in Nelspruit. Currently I'm working as a Manager at a Restaurant in Sabie. I decided to do a Bootcamp in Web Development at HyperionDev and hope to get a job in the field of Web Development.
